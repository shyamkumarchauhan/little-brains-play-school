Welcome to Little Brains Play School

Little Brains Play School was established with a vision to provide quality early childhood education in the Badarpur area of New Delhi. We believe that the first few years of a child's life are the most critical for their overall development.

Our school is designed to be a home away from home where children feel safe, loved, and inspired to learn. We combine structured curriculum with free play to ensure holistic development of every child.

Located at Shakti Vihar, Meethapur, Badarpur, we serve families from the surrounding communities with dedication and passion for early education.

🌟

Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To nurture every child's natural curiosity and love for learning through a safe, stimulating, and caring environment that fosters creativity, confidence, and character.

👁

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred preschool in Badarpur, known for excellence in early childhood education and producing confident, well-rounded young learners.

💓

Our Values

Love, respect, creativity, inclusivity, and excellence guide everything we do. We treat every child as unique and celebrate their individual strengths and talents.

🛠

Our Approach

We use a play-based, activity-driven methodology blending Montessori and modern teaching methods to ensure joyful, meaningful learning experiences for every student.
