Age-Appropriate Learning Programs

Every program is thoughtfully designed to match your child's developmental stage and bring out the best in them

👶

👶 Playgroup

Age: 2 - 3 Years

Our Playgroup program is designed to provide a gentle and loving introduction to school life. Children learn through sensory exploration, free play, songs, and simple activities that develop their motor skills and social awareness.

Key Focus Areas:

🎸
Music & Rhymes
🎨
Sensory Play
🤝
Social Skills
💪
Motor Development
🌱

🌱 Nursery

Age: 3 - 4 Years

The Nursery program builds on the foundations of Playgroup and introduces more structured learning activities. Children begin to recognize letters, numbers, shapes, and colors while developing their language and communication skills.

Key Focus Areas:

📜
Early Literacy
🔢
Number Recognition
🎨
Art & Craft
🗣
Language Skills
📗

📗 LKG (Lower Kindergarten)

Age: 4 - 5 Years

In LKG, children advance to reading and writing readiness. The curriculum includes phonics, basic math concepts, science exploration, and environmental awareness. Interactive activities keep learning fun and engaging.

Key Focus Areas:

📖
Phonics & Reading
➕
Basic Mathematics
🔬
Science Discovery
🌍
Environmental Awareness
🎓

🎓 UKG (Upper Kindergarten)

Age: 5 - 6 Years

UKG is the bridge to formal schooling. Children develop strong reading, writing, and mathematical skills. The program also focuses on building confidence, leadership qualities, and preparing children for the transition to primary school.

Key Focus Areas:

✍
Writing Skills
👥
Team Activities
💡
Critical Thinking
🏆
School Readiness
