About Little Brains Play School

Nurturing young minds with love, creativity, and a world of learning

🏫

A Safe Haven for Young Learners

At Little Brains Play School, we believe in creating a nurturing and stimulating environment where every child can explore, learn, and grow. Our experienced educators use play-based learning methods to develop your child's cognitive, social, and emotional skills.

Located in the heart of Badarpur, New Delhi, we provide a safe, clean, and joyful space for children ages 2 to 6 years.
